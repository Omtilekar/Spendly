Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided during registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the Service, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Service. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports within the app.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. We may use limited third-party infrastructure services (such as hosting providers) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations.

We do not integrate with third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media services.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us via the information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.

For details on how the Service may be used, see our Terms and Conditions.
